About
CGC Jhanjeri

Located in the heart of Punjab, Chandigarh Group of Colleges (CGC) Jhanjeri is a premier educational institution dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering innovation. Our state-of-the-art campus, world-class faculty, and commitment to excellence make CGC Jhanjeri a hub for aspiring professionals and entrepreneurs.

Introducing VentureNest

At CGC Jhanjeri, we have established VentureNest, a Section 8 company and Technology Business Incubator Association. VentureNest aims to create a supportive ecosystem for budding entrepreneurs, fostering creativity, and guiding startups towards success in the dynamic landscape of technology-driven business ventures. By collaborating with key regional and national startup ecosystem enablers, VentureNest provides startups with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities they need to thrive.

Venture Vault: Fueling Dreams, Funding Reality

VentureNest is proud to present Venture Vault a national-level event inspired by the popular SHARK TANK INDIA. Venture Vault is designed to be a premier platform for showcasing innovative ideas, connecting with investors, and celebrating entrepreneurial talent from across the nation. This event aims to build and strengthen the startup ecosystem in the region, providing unparalleled opportunities for startups to grow and succeed.

Event Segments

Pitch Deck Competition

Showcase your innovative ideas to a panel of seasoned investors and industry experts for a chance to secure funding and mentorship. This competition will highlight the brightest minds and the most promising startups, giving them a platform to present their business concepts and strategies.

Start-up Exhibition

Witness the latest innovations and entrepreneurial ventures from top talents across the nation, including esteemed participants from nearby schools and colleges. The exhibition will feature a diverse range of startups, offering attendees a glimpse into the future of various industries.

Keynote Sessions & Panel Discussions

Gain insights from renowned thought leaders, successful entrepreneurs, and investors through enlightening keynote sessions and engaging panel discussions. These sessions will cover a wide array of topics, providing valuable knowledge and inspiration to aspiring entrepreneurs and established business leaders alike.

Investors Meet & Networking

Connect with potential investors, mentors, and industry leaders to explore collaboration opportunities and expand your professional network. This segment is designed to facilitate meaningful interactions, helping startups find the right partners and resources to accelerate their growth.

Awards for the Top Rising Start-ups

Celebrate the top rising startups and visionary entrepreneurs who are shaping the future of innovation. Awards will be presented in various categories, recognizing outstanding achievements and groundbreaking ideas that have the potential to transform industries.

Building the Startup Ecosystem

Venture Vault aims to create a thriving startup ecosystem in the region by:

  • Providing Exposure: Startups will gain visibility and recognition on a national platform, attracting potential investors, partners, and customers.

  • Facilitating Growth: Through mentorship, networking, and funding opportunities, startups will receive the support they need to grow and scale their businesses.

  • Fostering Innovation: The event will inspire creativity and innovation, encouraging more individuals to pursue entrepreneurial ventures.

  • Strengthening Collaboration: By bringing together startups, investors, mentors, and industry experts, VENTUREVAULT will foster a collaborative environment where ideas can flourish and partnerships can thrive.
